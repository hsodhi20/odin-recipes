Amritsari Lassi

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups plain yogurt
  • 1/4 cup sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1/2 cup chilled milk
  • 1/4 teaspoon cardamom powder
  • A pinch of saffron strands (optional)
  • Crushed ice, for serving
  • Chopped pistachios or almonds, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a blender, add the plain yogurt, sugar, chilled milk, cardamom powder, and saffron strands (if using).
  2. Blend everything together until smooth and frothy.
  3. Transfer the lassi to serving glasses.
  4. Add crushed ice on top.
  5. Garnish with chopped pistachios or almonds.
  6. Serve the Amritsari Lassi chilled and enjoy!
